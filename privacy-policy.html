Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

SparkleHeaven respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or book our services, we may collect:

  • •Contact Details: Your name, phone number, and email address shared via our contact forms or WhatsApp.
  • •Event Details: Information about your event such as date, venue location, guest count, and service preferences.
  • •Usage Data: Basic browser information and website interaction data collected via standard analytics tools.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • •Respond to your service inquiries and provide price quotes.
  • •Coordinate and manage your event bookings.
  • •Send important updates regarding your event.
  • •Improve our website and services based on user feedback.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary details with trusted third-party partners (like catering teams or photographers) only as required to fulfill your event booking. Your data is never shared for third-party marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or to request correction or deletion of that data. Contact us directly to exercise these rights.
